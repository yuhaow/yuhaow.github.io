Yuhao Wang (王禹皓)

 

Welcome to my homepage!

I am an assistant professor in the Institute of Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University. Before that, I was a postdoctoral research associate at the Statistical Laboratory, which is part of the Department of Pure Mathematics and Mathematical Statistics at the University of Cambridge. My postdoc advisor was Dr. Rajen Shah.

I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at MIT. My advisor was Prof. Caroline Uhler. Prior to my Ph.D., I got my bachelor from the Department of Automation in Tsinghua University.
