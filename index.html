Yuhao Wang | 王禹皓

 

Welcome to my homepage!

I am an assistant professor in the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University. I am also affiliated with Shanghai Qi Zhi Institute. Before joining Tsinghua, I was a postdoctoral research associate in the Statistical Laboratory at the University of Cambridge, where I was supervised by Professor Rajen Shah.

I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Professor Caroline Uhler. During my Ph.D., I was affiliated with the Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems (LIDS). Prior to my Ph.D., I got my bachelor from the Department of Automation in Tsinghua University.
